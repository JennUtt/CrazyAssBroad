New Year, New You?

    by Jenn
    published on January 1, 2018

    It seems like Americans, on social media, are more pessimistic than usual this year. "New Year, New You...YEAH RIGHT!" More than worrying about something to resolve, we should really try to adjust our attitude towards adjusting to change. People fear change because they don't like feeling uncomfortable. This year is going to be a year of adjustments and reflection. 

    The goal for this broad is going to post at least once a day, everyday, for an entire year. This will give some reflection for the 2018 year and give me, and maybe you, a chance to reflect change. Even if we don't like it, outside influences are constantly changing our perspective and personality. It's how we adjust to those changes that will reflect our character.
